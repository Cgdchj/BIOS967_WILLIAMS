Readme for Logger_3_Edited_Local

This is data on incubation temperatures for a Killdeer nest with 2 eggs at Sandy Pointe Lake in Ashland, Nebraska. The data was collected using an ibutton placed inside the nest on 06/13/2023 at 10:30am with an ambient temperature of 23.33 Celcius. Readings were collected every 10 minutes. The ibutton ran out of space before it was removed, but at the time of removal, the nest contained 3 eggs, and the ibutton was moved outside of the nest; it was originally placed under an egg. 

Variables:
Date/time: Date and time of data point
Unit: Celcius 
Value: Temperature of nest
