Readme for Logger 2_Edited_Local

This is data on incubation temperatures for a Killdeer nest with 1 egg at Ritz Lake housing development in Fremont, Nebraska. The data was collected using an ibutton placed inside the nest on 06/12/2023 at 13:54pm with an ambient temperature of 22.77 Celsius. Readings were collected every 10 minutes. The Killdeer was observed back on the nest at 14:35pm. The ibutton ran out  of space before it was removed, but at time of removal, the nest contained 4 eggs.

Variables:
Date/time: date and time of data point
Unit: Celsius
Value: Temperature of nest
